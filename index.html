MISSION BRIEFING

Tax evaders have gone rogue. As Shane Ong, assemble a squad of 3 agents and shut them down. Each run takes about 5 minutes.

HOW TO PLAY

• Pick 3 agents and set their turn order
• Each agent has 5 AP for the whole mission (4 AP on Hard)
• Skills cost 1–2 AP. Basic attack is always free
• Choose a skill, then click an enemy to target
• Survive 2 waves + the boss to win
• If a wave drags past 5 rounds without enemies dying, they ENRAGE — permanent +20% ATK. Deal damage fast.
